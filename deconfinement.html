Déconfinement (2021)

video, 4'00".

Déconfinement is picturing the flow of lives at the end of the pandemic of COVID-19. The video combined images in Lozère, an area in France, with sound recorded in a folk event in Taipei, Taiwan. This is a video made for the exhibition "Cou Cou" at La Ronceraie, Cans et Cévennes, France.
