project beautiful pictures (2016)

website.

project beautiful pictures is based on 500 photographs, fonud in an abandoned ruins of marine technician dormatory in south of Taiwan. Taken around 1980, these photos are mostly amateur staged portraits of women. The men who resided in this dorm came to Taiwan due to the historical event, Da-Chen Island Evacuation, which is part of the retreat of the regime Republic of China from mainland China to Taiwan after Chinese Civil War. project beautiful pictures is followed by one who opposed to be framed being trained, while the other one being deprive of comfortable prepare (2016)
