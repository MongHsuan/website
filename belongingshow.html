Don't forget your personal belongings. (2018)

site-specific installation with photo prints.
collaborated with Nolwenn Salaun.

Don't forget your personal belongings is a site-specific installation at de kijkdoos in Amsterdam. "De kijkdoos" means boxes to look into in Dutch. I brought my photo archive to the space, selected and installed images in accordance to the space, the soundscape, flow of daylight and the equipped florescent light inside the boxes.
