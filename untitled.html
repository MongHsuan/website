Untitled (2018)

all photographs are chromogenic prints with Taiwanese wooden frames without glasses.

the pride, her inferior pride,
oversaturated in the water of the sun.
a slide, a gap, to a soft fall.
a lift, a bow, being blown off.
a climb, a bull, and a sneaking lost.
here the heart stroke.
all is for having you in front, in tone.
